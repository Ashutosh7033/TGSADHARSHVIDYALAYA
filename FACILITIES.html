Indoor Games & Activities

We offer a variety of indoor games such as table tennis, carrom, and chess, providing students with opportunities to relax and recharge. These activities are carefully selected to develop strategic thinking, teamwork, and problem-solving skills in a fun and engaging manner.

Weekend Outdoor Sports with Professional Coaching

For students who love the outdoors, we organize weekend sports activities including cricket, football, badminton, basketball, and swimming. These activities are conducted under the guidance of experienced coaches, ensuring proper training and development, making weekends both exciting and enriching for our students.

To assist students who may find it challenging to keep up with the academic demands, we offer a specially designed Bridge Course. Without losing a year, students are given the support they need to build confidence, align with their peers, and meet the challenges of the next academic level.
